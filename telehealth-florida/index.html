Common Questions

Is MedCare licensed to practice in Florida?

Yes. MedCare operates under Medcare Telehealth Inc., a Florida corporation licensed to provide telehealth services throughout the state.

Do I need to be physically in Florida for my visit?

Yes. You must be physically located in Florida at the time of your telehealth visit, per Florida state law.

Can I get a prescription sent to a Florida pharmacy?

Yes. We send prescriptions to any pharmacy in Florida, including CVS, Walgreens, Publix, and compounding pharmacies.
