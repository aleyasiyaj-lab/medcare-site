FAQ

Common Questions

What urgent care conditions can be treated online in Florida?

UTIs, sinus infections, strep throat, ear infections, skin rashes, pink eye, and other common non-emergency conditions can typically be evaluated and treated via telehealth.

Is telehealth urgent care legal in Florida?

Yes. Florida has strong telehealth laws that allow licensed providers to evaluate and treat patients — including prescribing medications — via telemedicine.

What if I need in-person care?

If your condition needs an in-person exam or emergency care, we'll direct you to the appropriate resource immediately.

How do I get started?

Click "Book a Visit," fill out a short intake form, and we'll confirm your appointment within 2 hours. Visits are conducted by phone or video — no app download required.
