What Is Pink Eye and Why Does It Need Attention?

Pink eye, also known as conjunctivitis, is an inflammation or infection of the thin, clear tissue that lines the inside of your eyelid and covers the white part of your eye. While it is usually not serious, it can be uncomfortable and highly contagious. The most common symptoms include redness, itching, a gritty feeling, discharge that may form a crust overnight, and tearing. Because pink eye can be caused by viruses, bacteria, or allergies, getting a proper evaluation is key to knowing how to manage it.

Seeking prompt care can help relieve symptoms sooner, reduce the risk of spreading it to family or coworkers, and rule out more serious eye conditions. That is why an online visit with a knowledgeable provider is an ideal first step.

When Should You Choose an Online Visit for Pink Eye?

Virtual urgent care is an excellent option for many cases of pink eye, especially if you have mild to moderate symptoms and no serious underlying eye conditions. Pink eye treatment online is particularly helpful for:

  • Parents with a child who wakes up with sticky eyes and needs a quick note for school or daycare.
  • Adults who notice redness and irritation but cannot take time off work for an in-person appointment.
  • Anyone who wants to avoid exposing others to a contagious illness by staying out of waiting rooms.
  • Patients who have had pink eye before and recognize the symptoms early.

However, if you experience severe eye pain, sudden vision changes, sensitivity to light, or if you have a history of eye injury or surgery, it is important to seek in-person emergency care. Our NP will help guide you on whether a virtual visit is right for your specific situation.
